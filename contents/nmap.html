Options

Options Descriptions
-sT TCP connect scan (SYN, SYN-ACK, ACK, RESET)
-sS TCP SYN scan (SYN, SYN-ACK, RESET)
-sA TCP ACK scan (Only ACK flag -- can't distinguish whether ports are open or closed)
-sU UDP scan
-p [PORT RANGES] Port specification, i.e. -p22; -p1-65535; -p U:53,111,137,T:21-25,80,139,8080,S:9
-O Enables OS detection
-A enable OS detection along with other things (incl. versions)
-sV Enables version detection
--script
[FILENAME|CATEGORY|DIRECTORY|EXPRESSSION]		 Runs a script scan
--script=banner: banner scan
